Our Mission

About Us

Women.kg is more than just a platform; it is a community-driven initiative at AUCA designed to empower women through resources, mentorship, and support. Our mission is to provide a safe space for personal and professional growth, fostering the next generation of female leaders in Kyrgyzstan.
